MyController is a flexible automation and sensor controller for IoT devices.
Whether in the home, at work, or wherever you need it, MyController provides the solution!

Privacy focused - working locally and not in the cloud means your data stays safely with you. Fully functional out of the box. No internet connection needed to run.
Lightweight - runs on both Windows and Linux including Raspberry Pi zero, 1, 2, 3, and 4. Requires only 50MB of memory and 100MB of disk.
